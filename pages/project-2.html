Battleship

Online multiplayer alternative version of Battleship. The game was completed in a team of four students as a two month long project in CSCE 315: Programming Studio. Unlike standard Battleship rules, our version has both players playing simultaneously while also adding a resource management system. Standard Battleship has no defensive play other than the initial ship placement. As such, the ability for players to move their ships as well as fire at their opponents was added. Overall, I believe that these additions added meaningful decision making between both players.
